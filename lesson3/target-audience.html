This site is for anyone who wants to be able to easily know what the weather is going to be like.

First persona: People wanting to learn about the local economy, and about different businesses in the area.

Second persona: Businesses who may have concerns about something with the local economy, or who want to learn about things that are happening locally.

Scenarios:

  • Someone needs to find out how to get a license to start a business
  • A business wants to learn more about how they can be more active in the local economy
  • Someone wants to learn more about local businesses
  • A business wants to know what laws there are for the local economy
  • A city wants to help other people find information about the local economy
